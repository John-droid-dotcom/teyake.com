General Information

Who can use Tyake.com?

Tyake.com is a website for teachers who want to carry out digital examinations with their students or who want give examinations in some unfavourable environment for physical examination.

How do i register a teacher account?

Go to home page and register via institutional email, by entering all the necessary information.

Do my students also create account on Tyake.com?

No they don't. Instead, students access the exam using Exam Key.The teacher sends as soon as he/she creates an exam on Tyake.com

Sign in

Teachers can sign in to Tyake.com with an email and password or with one of the following methods.

Microsoft

To sign in through Microsoft, click on the "sign in with Microsoft" button. As a user, you will first be redirected to Microsoft. If you are already logged in,you will automatically be sent back to Tyake.com.

Google

To sign in through the Google integration, click on the "sign in with Google" button.

Username and Password

In addition to the above ways, the user can also sign in with an email and password. if You forget your password,you can create a new one through a link that is sent to your email address. All passwords are stored in a protected environment

Create an exam

You can create an exam through entering the questions and answers to the space provided.you can select the specifications found there.

Unique identification codes

The identity codes are unique. in other words, no two students can have the same code.

During the exam

What does exam opened/closed mean

An open exam can be found and started immediatedy by the students by using the exam key.

The exam is closed mean that the key is not valid anymore and can't be used to find the exam

What happens if the student looses unternet connection during the exam?

If students lose internet connection during the exam, notification will be shown to bothe the student and the teacher. however, the student can continue to write without interruption. Then, when the internet is back, the text will be automatically synced with the server.

what happens if the student's computer runs out of battery?

If the student's computer stops working, the student can resume the exam from another device using the exam key

Security Levels

Tyake.com is a highly secured website with different security measures. It will be difficult for the student to cheat during the exam, one because there will not be enough time to search and two because the student's activity will be tracked, like when he/she opens other tabs.
