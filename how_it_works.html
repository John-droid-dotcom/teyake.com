How It Works

Its easy to use Teyake.com

Create an Exam
  1. Create an Account as a Teacher
  2. From the Teacher Dashboard, Click the Create Exam Button from the Tabs
  3. Enter Exam Information
  4. Enter Questions and Answers of your Exam
  5. Specify Rules and Enter Points of Your Questions
  6. Finally Release Your Exam Whenever You Like by Shifting the Toggle

Take an Exam
  1. Receive an Exam Key from the Exam Giver
  2. Enter Exam Key and Fetch the Exam
  3. Enter Required Fields In order to Begin the Exam
  4. Take Your Exam Accordingly
  5. Submit your Exam When Finished

Monitor Exams
  1. Create an Account as a Teacher
  2. Create Your Exam
  3. Go to Exam List
  4. Enter your Desired Exam
  5. Manipulate and Change Settings as you wish
